ARTIST

SHAMMY IS A KENYAN-BORN ARTIST FOCUSING ON FINE ART, PHOTOGRAPHY, FILM AND ARCHITECTURE. SHAMMY HAS USED HIS CHILDHOOD LOVE FOR DRAWING AND DIGITAL ART TO CREATE ARTISTIC EXPRESSIONS AND ARTWORK WHICH SERVE AS A SYMBOL OF DIVERSITY. SHAMMY'S WORK HAS FEATURED ACROSS DIFFERENT ART MAGAZINES AND EXHIBITIONS. SHAMMY'S GALLERY, BY SHAMMY, IS A CONTEMPORARY GALLERY FEATURING SHAMMY AND HIS WORKS.
