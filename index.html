Shammy

Brighton, United Kingdom

Shamilton(Shammy) is a mixed media artist mainly focusing on fine art, photograpihy and film. Hailing from the vibrant city of Nairobi, Kenya, and currently living in England, Shammy has used his childhood love for drawing and digital art to create artistic expressions and create pieces which are a symbol of diversity. His work has caught the eye of organisations such as the United Nations and featured across different exhibitions both in Africa and Europe. After starting off in his early years as a traditional artist, focusing mostly on charcoal, pencil art and paint/graffiti, he later transitioned to photography, film and Architecture.
